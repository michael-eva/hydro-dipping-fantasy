About Us

My name is Michael and I am the founder of Galactic Shark Hydrographics.

We manage and run and information site that happens to sell hydro-graphics products and accessories.

As we are starting out the business, we are aiming at the project level buyers but as the business gains momentum, we will start exploring the commercial market as well, being careful not to neglect the project or DIY market.

- We provide as much information as we have access to without hiding anything and having to make the buyer purchase something unnecessarily.

- We also provide the products and accessories at a lower price compared to our competitors.

- 15% of our profits go to helping to save the ocean.

Story

I noticed that there is very little information around on hydro-dipping and you have to buy all these weird products and kits just to get started. This made me feel a bit deflated because it all seemed so expensive. I realised that if I feel this way, there must be other people who feel like this too, so I decided that I would try my best to provide as much information as I possibly can about hydro-dipping.

Mission

Our mission here at *hydro-dipping-fantasy is to provide as much information as we possibly can about hydro-dipping and the hydro-dipping process while also offering the most inexpensive film and accessories.

Vision

- We envision more people making their projects personalised with the use of hydro-dipping to give their project an exciting new look.

- With regards to the ocean, we just want people to stop being dicks to the ocean and realising that without the ocean ecosystem, most of our oxygen producing plants will be dead as well as a major source of food.

What do you want me to do?

I encourage you to have a look around the website and see what information we have to offer and hopefully it piques your interest and makes you want to tackle a project of your own. If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to get in contact with me!
